Synthetic gloves are made from materials other than natural rubber, such as vinyl or polyethylene. Polyisoprene (PI) gloves offer a latex-like fit and feel without the risk of latex allergies. Neoprene gloves offer chemical resistance, while nitrile gloves are known for their puncture resistance and durability.</p></p>

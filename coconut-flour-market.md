<p><p>Coconut flour can be classified into three main types based on fat content and fiber concentration. Whole full-fat coconut flour retains all the natural fats in coconut, providing a rich and flavorful ingredient. Low-fat high-fiber coconut flour is produced by removing some fat content but retaining high fiber levels. Medium-fat coconut flour strikes a balance between fat content and fiber concentration, offering a versatile option for various baking needs. Each type caters to different dietary preferences and culinary requirements.</p></p>

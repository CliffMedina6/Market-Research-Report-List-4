<p><p>Heat transfer paper and vinyl are materials used in the process of creating custom designs on various types of fabrics and surfaces. Heat transfer paper is a special type of paper that is printed with a design using an inkjet or laser printer. The design is then transferred onto the fabric using heat and pressure from a heat press machine. Heat transfer vinyl, on the other hand, is a type of material that is cut into a design using a vinyl cutter and then also applied to the fabric using heat and pressure.</p><p>The Heat Transfer Paper and Vinyl Market is witnessing steady growth due to the increasing demand for custom-designed apparel and promotional items.

<p><p>Solid state timers can be classified into two main types based on their external resistor configuration: External Resistor Adjustable and External Resistor Fixed. In the External Resistor Adjustable market, users have the flexibility to change the timing settings by adjusting the external resistor values. On the other hand, in the External Resistor Fixed market, the timing settings are predetermined and cannot be adjusted by the user. Both types offer different levels of customization and are suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>

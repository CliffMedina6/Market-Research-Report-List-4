<p><p>Vertical auger model hand juicers are designed with a vertical auger mechanism that efficiently extracts juice from fruits and vegetables by crushing and pressing them. These juicers are known for their high juice yield and ability to extract juice from leafy greens. On the other hand, horizontal auger model hand juicers have a horizontal auger mechanism that squeezes and grinds produce to extract juice. They are versatile and can handle a wide range of fruits and vegetables, making them popular among consumers looking for a multipurpose juicing solution.</p></p>

<p><p>Open peripheral pumps are versatile pumping systems that come in various types to suit different applications. Two common market types are the magnetic peripheral pump and the centrifugal peripheral pump. </p><p>Magnetic peripheral pumps utilize magnetic force to drive the impeller, resulting in efficient and maintenance-free operation. </p><p>On the other hand, centrifugal peripheral pumps rely on centrifugal force to move fluid through the system, making them a popular choice for applications requiring high flow rates and moderate pressure. Both types offer reliable performance and are widely used in various industries.</p></p>

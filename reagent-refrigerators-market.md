<p><p>Reagent refrigerators are classified into different types based on the temperature range they can achieve. Low temperature refrigerators typically maintain temperatures between 2-8 degrees Celsius, suitable for storing most common reagents and samples. Ultra-low temperature refrigerators, on the other hand, maintain temperatures below 0 degrees Celsius, making them ideal for storing sensitive materials such as vaccines and enzymes. Other market types may include specialty refrigerators designed for specific applications, such as cryogenic storage or environmental chambers for temperature and humidity control.</p></p>

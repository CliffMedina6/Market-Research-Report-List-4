<p><p>Automotive Safety Electronics Market comprises two main types - systems and sensors. These systems work together to prevent accidents and protect passengers in the event of a crash. On the other hand, safety sensors detect and relay information to the safety systems, such as radar sensors for blind spot monitoring and cameras for lane departure warning systems. Both systems and sensors play crucial roles in enhancing automotive safety and preventing accidents.</p></p>

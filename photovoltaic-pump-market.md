DC Surface Suction pumps are ideal for areas with shallow water sources, while AC Submersible pumps are submerged in deep water sources. DC Submersible pumps are used in off-grid locations where electricity is limited. AC Floating pumps are designed for water bodies where the pump needs to stay afloat.

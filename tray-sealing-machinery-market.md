<p><p>Tray sealing machinery is available in different types, including manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual tray sealing machines require operators to manually load trays and seal them. Semi-automatic machines automate the sealing process but still require some manual intervention. Fully automatic machines are fully automated and require minimal human intervention, as they can load trays, seal them, and handle the entire process. Each type caters to different production volumes and levels of automation, offering flexibility and efficiency in packaging operations.</p></p>

<p><p>Smart electronic scales come in two main types based on their connectivity options: Wifi connection and Bluetooth connection. Wifi-enabled scales allow users to connect their scale to their home network, enabling data syncing to cloud services and access to a wider range of features. On the other hand, Bluetooth-connected scales require a direct connection to a smartphone or tablet for data transmission, offering more convenient and immediate access to data through dedicated apps. Both options provide users with advanced tracking and monitoring capabilities for their weight and health metrics.</p></p>

<p><p>Active wheelchair market consists of two main types: manual wheelchairs and power wheelchairs. Manual wheelchairs are propelled by the user through hand rims attached to the wheels, providing exercise and independence. They are suitable for users with good upper body strength and mobility. Power wheelchairs, on the other hand, are electrically powered and controlled using a joystick or other input device. These are ideal for users with limited mobility or strength, providing greater independence and ease of use. Both types of wheelchairs offer different features and benefits to cater to the diverse needs of individuals with mobility challenges.</p></p>

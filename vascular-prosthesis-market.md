<p><p>The vascular prosthesis market includes various types of grafts such as polyester grafts, ePTFE (expanded polytetrafluoroethylene) grafts, polyurethane grafts, and biosynthetic grafts. Polyester grafts are durable and widely used for vascular surgeries. ePTFE grafts have a low infection rate and high patency. Polyurethane grafts are flexible and biocompatible. Biosynthetic grafts are made from natural materials and promote tissue ingrowth. Each type of graft offers unique benefits and is used according to the specific needs of the patient.</p></p>

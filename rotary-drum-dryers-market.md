<p><p>Rotary drum dryers are used in industries to dry materials by tumbling them in a rotating drum. There are two main types of rotary drum dryers based on how heat is applied: directly heated and indirectly heated. In directly heated dryers, the material comes in direct contact with hot gas or air to remove moisture. In indirectly heated dryers, heat is transferred to the material through a metal wall, avoiding direct contact with the heating source. Each type has its specific applications and advantages in different industries.</p></p>

<p><p>Benchtop electronic nose market types include metal oxide, electrochemical, conductive polymer, photoionizing, and others. Metal oxide electronic noses utilize semiconductor sensors for gas detection, while electrochemical types use electrodes to detect gases. Conductive polymer electronic noses rely on changes in conductivity to identify odors, while photoionizing types detect volatile organic compounds through ionization. Other electronic nose types may use different sensing technologies such as surface acoustic wave sensors or optical sensors for odor detection.</p></p>

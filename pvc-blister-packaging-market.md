<p><strong>The PVC Blister Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoforming</li><li>Cold Forming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVC blister packaging is available in two main types: thermoforming and cold forming. Thermoforming involves heating a PVC sheet until it becomes pliable, then forming it into a blister shape using a mold. Cold forming, on the other hand, does not require heat and instead relies on pressure to shape the PVC into a blister pack. Both methods are commonly used in the pharmaceutical, food, and cosmetic industries for packaging products securely and displaying them attractively.</p></p>

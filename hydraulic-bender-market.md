<p><ul><li>Torsional Axis Synchronous</li><li>Synchronous Machine Liquid</li><li>Electro-Hydraulic Synchronization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydraulic benders are commonly categorized into three types based on their synchronization mechanism: Torsional Axis Synchronous, Synchronous Machine Liquid, and Electro-Hydraulic Synchronization. Torsional Axis Synchronous benders use a torsional axis for synchronization, while Synchronous Machine Liquid benders utilize a liquid medium for synchronization. Electro-Hydraulic Synchronization benders rely on an electro-hydraulic system for synchronization. Each type offers unique advantages and applications, catering to different needs in the market for hydraulic bending equipment.</p></p>

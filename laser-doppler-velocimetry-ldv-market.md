<p><p>Laser Doppler Velocimetry (LDV) measures fluid flow velocity by analyzing the Doppler shift of laser light scattered by particles in the flow. The two main types of LDV receivers are Forward Scatter Receiver, which detects light scattered at small angles in the direction of the laser beam, and Backward Scatter Receiver, which detects light scattered at large angles opposite to the laser beam. These receivers allow for precise and non-intrusive measurement of flow velocity in various applications such as aerospace, automotive, and medical research.</p></p>

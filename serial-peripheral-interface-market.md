<p><p>The Serial Peripheral Interface market can be categorized into Active Mode and Passive Mode depending on the involvement of the device in data transfer. Active Mode devices typically require power to operate and have more control over the communication process, while Passive Mode devices rely on external signals to receive and transmit data.

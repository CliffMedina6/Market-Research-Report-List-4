<p><p>Thermogravimetric Analyzer (TGA) market consists of two main types: High Pressure TGA and Atmospheric TGA. High Pressure TGA is used for studying materials under high pressure conditions, providing insights into how materials behave in extreme environments. On the other hand, Atmospheric TGA is used for analyzing materials at normal atmospheric pressure, allowing researchers to investigate the thermal decomposition of various substances. Both types of TGA are essential tools in materials research, quality control, and process optimization in various industries.</p></p>

<p><p>Industrial Radiography Testing is a non-destructive testing method used to detect defects in structures and materials using radiation sources. The four main types of Industrial Radiography Testing methods are Film Radiography, Computed Radiography, Direct Radiography, and Computed Tomography. Film Radiography uses photographic film to capture images, Computed Radiography converts radiation images into digital form, Direct Radiography uses digital detectors to capture images, and Computed Tomography creates cross-sectional images of objects for a more detailed analysis. Each method has its own advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>

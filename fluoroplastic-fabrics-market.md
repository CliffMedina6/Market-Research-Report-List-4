<p><p>Fluoroplastic fabrics are widely used in various industries for their exceptional chemical resistance and durability. The two main types of fluoroplastic fabrics are plain fabrics and mesh. Plain fabrics are solid and denser, providing a barrier against liquids and gases. Mesh fabrics, on the other hand, have a more open and breathable structure, allowing for air and liquid flow while still maintaining chemical resistance. Both types of fabrics offer unique benefits and are chosen based on the specific needs of the application.</p></p>

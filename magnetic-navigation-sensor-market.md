<p><p>The Magnetic Navigation Sensor Market includes three main types: AMR (Anisotropic Magnetoresistance Effect), GMR (Giant Magnetoresistance Effect), and TMR (Tunnel Magnetoresistance Effect). These sensors utilize different magnetic effects to detect changes in magnetic fields and provide highly accurate navigation capabilities for various applications. AMR sensors offer good resolution and stability, GMR sensors provide high sensitivity, and TMR sensors offer the highest sensitivity and signal-to-noise ratio. Each type of sensor has its own advantages and is used in different navigation systems based on specific requirements.</p></p>

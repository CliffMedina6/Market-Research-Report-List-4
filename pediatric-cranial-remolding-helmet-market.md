<p><p>Pediatric Cranial Remolding Helmets are used to treat various head shape abnormalities in infants and young children. Plagiocephaly is characterized by a flat spot on one side of the head, while Brachycephaly involves a disproportionately wide head shape. Scaphocephaly results in a long and narrow head shape, and Craniosynostosis occurs when the bones in an infant's skull fuse together too early. Each type of helmet is designed to gently reshape the skull over time, correcting the specific abnormality.</p></p>

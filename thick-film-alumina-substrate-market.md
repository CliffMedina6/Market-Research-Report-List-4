<p><p>The thick film alumina substrate market offers two main types: Multi Layer and Single Layer. Multi Layer substrates contain multiple layers of conductive and insulating materials stacked on top of each other, providing better performance and higher integration capabilities. Single Layer substrates, on the other hand, consist of only one layer of conductive and insulating materials, offering simpler and more cost-effective solutions. Both types cater to different needs and applications in the electronic industry based on their specific characteristics and functionalities.</p></p>

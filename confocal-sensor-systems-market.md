<p><p>Confocal sensor systems are used for high-precision measurement and inspection in various industries. Point sensors focus on measuring a single point on an object, providing precise data on dimensions or surface texture. Multipoint sensors are capable of measuring multiple points simultaneously, increasing efficiency in inspection processes. 3D line confocal sensors scan a line on the object surface to create a detailed 3D profile, allowing for thorough analysis of complex shapes. These different types cater to the diverse needs of industries requiring precise and reliable measurement solutions.</p></p>

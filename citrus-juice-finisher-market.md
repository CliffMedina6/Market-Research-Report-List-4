<p><ul><li>Screw-Based</li><li>Paddle Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The citrus juice finisher market can be categorized into two main types: screw-based and paddle-based finishers. Screw-based finishers use a rotating screw mechanism to separate juice from pulp, while paddle-based finishers use paddles to agitate the mixture and separate the juice. Screw-based finishers are typically more efficient and suitable for larger-scale operations, while paddle-based finishers are often used for smaller batches or specialty citrus juices. Both types of finishers play a crucial role in the citrus juice production process by ensuring high-quality juice extraction.</p></p>

<p><p>The Battery Cell Bypass Switch market can be classified into two main types: Static Type and Manual Type. Static Type switches automatically divert power flow to bypass a faulty cell in a battery system, ensuring uninterrupted operation. On the other hand, Manual Type switches require human intervention to manually switch power flow to bypass the faulty cell. Both types offer solutions for managing battery cell health and optimizing overall system performance in various applications.</p></p>

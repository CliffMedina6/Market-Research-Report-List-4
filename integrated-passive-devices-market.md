<p><p>Integrated Passive Devices (IPD) market can be divided into several types based on their specific functions. This includes Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) protection, Electromagnetic Interference (EMI) suppression, Radio Frequency (RF) IPD for wireless communication applications, and other miscellaneous IPDs for various functionalities. ESD IPDs safeguard electronic components from damage caused by electrostatic discharge, while EMI IPDs minimize electromagnetic interference in circuit designs. RF-IPDs are specifically designed for efficient wireless communication systems. Other IPDs serve different purposes such as impedance matching and filtering in electronic devices.</p></p>

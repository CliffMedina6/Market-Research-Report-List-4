<p><strong>The Kitchen Electric Water Heater Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tank</li><li>Tankless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The kitchen electric water heater market consists of two main types: tank and tankless heaters. Tank water heaters store and heat water in a large tank, ensuring a constant supply of hot water. Tankless water heaters heat water on demand, eliminating the need for a storage tank and providing a more energy-efficient solution. Both types cater to different consumer needs, with tank heaters being more suitable for larger households with high water demands, while tankless heaters are ideal for smaller households looking to save space and energy.</p></p>

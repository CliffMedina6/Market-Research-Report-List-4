<p><p>The mosquito repellent market offers various types of products to help protect against the annoying and potentially harmful insects. These products include coils, vaporizers, mats, aerosols, and creams. Coils are burned to release a smoke that repels mosquitoes, while vaporizers heat up liquid repellent to create a protective vapor. Mats are infused with repellent and heated to release the active ingredients into the air. Aerosols are sprays that can be applied directly to the skin or clothing, while creams provide a barrier against mosquito bites.</p></p>

<p><p>Electromagnetic flowmeter converters are categorized into two main types- Integrated Type and Remote Type. Integrated Type combines the flow sensor and converter into a single unit, offering a compact and easy-to-install solution. On the other hand, Remote Type features a separate converter that can be installed at a different location than the flow sensor. This provides flexibility in installation and allows for remote monitoring and control. Both types cater to different needs and requirements in various industries where accurate flow measurement is crucial.</p></p>

Planar multi-joint robot controllers are specific to robots operating within a two-dimensional plane, making them suitable for tasks like pick-and-place operations. Coordinate robot controllers focus on precise positioning and movement of robots in a particular coordinate system. Each type of controller offers unique capabilities to meet specific requirements in the field of robotics.</p></p>

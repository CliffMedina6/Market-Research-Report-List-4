<p><p>API roller chains are commonly categorized into two market types: single strand and multi strand chains. Single strand chains consist of a single row of links, providing a simple and efficient solution for lighter duty applications. On the other hand, multi strand chains feature multiple rows of links running parallel to each other, offering increased strength and load capacity for heavier duty applications. Both types of chains play a crucial role in various industrial applications, providing reliable power transmission and motion control solutions.</p></p>

<p><p>HIC substrates are used in a variety of industries for their high thermal conductivity properties. Al2O3 HIC substrates are made from aluminum oxide and are known for their excellent insulation and resistance to high temperatures. On the other hand, AlN HIC substrates are made from aluminum nitride and offer even higher thermal conductivity, making them ideal for applications where heat dissipation is crucial. Both types of substrates are essential components in the manufacture of electronic devices and power modules.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Crystal Material</li><li>Polymer Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Infrared windows are divided into two main types based on the material used: Crystal material and polymer material. Crystal material windows are made from materials such as sapphire or germanium, known for their high transmission rates of infrared radiation. These windows are typically more expensive but offer better optical clarity and durability. On the other hand, polymer material windows are made from plastic materials like polyethylene or PTZ, which provide cost-effective solutions with good optical performance for infrared applications.</p></p>

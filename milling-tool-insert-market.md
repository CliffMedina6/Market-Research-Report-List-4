<p><p>Milling tool inserts are essential components in machining operations. There are three main types of inserts in the market: carbide tool inserts, CBN inserts, and ceramic inserts. Carbide inserts are known for their durability and versatility, making them suitable for a wide range of materials. CBN inserts are designed for high-speed machining of hardened steels and superalloys. Ceramic inserts are preferred for their high heat and wear resistance, making them ideal for high-speed cutting applications in difficult-to-machine materials.</p></p>

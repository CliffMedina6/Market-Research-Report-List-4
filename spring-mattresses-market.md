<p><strong>The Spring Mattresses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bonnell coils</li><li>Offset coils</li><li>Continuous coils</li><li>Marshall coils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spring mattresses come in various types based on the type of coils used in their construction. Bonnell coils are hourglass-shaped and interconnected, providing good support and durability. Offset coils have a square head shape for better contouring to the body. Continuous coils are made from a single piece of wire, offering consistent support. Marshall coils are individually wrapped coils that reduce motion transfer. Each type of coil offers different benefits in terms of support, durability, and motion isolation in the mattress market.</p></p>

<p><p>The Galvanometer Optical Scanner Market includes two main types: Single Axis and Dual Axis scanners. Single Axis scanners have one moving mirror that scans in one direction, while Dual Axis scanners have two moving mirrors that scan in both horizontal and vertical directions. Single Axis scanners are simpler and more cost-effective, suitable for basic applications. Dual Axis scanners offer higher precision and flexibility, making them ideal for more complex tasks such as laser engraving or 3D printing. Both types have their own advantages and cater to different market needs.</p></p>

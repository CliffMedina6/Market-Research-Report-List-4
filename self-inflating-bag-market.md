<p><p>A self-inflating bag is a portable device used in emergency situations to provide ventilation to patients who are unable to breathe on their own. It is commonly used in hospitals, clinics, ambulances, and other medical settings. The self-inflating bag consists of a bag reservoir, one-way valve, and a mask or tubing for the patient to breathe through. When squeezed, the bag reservoir fills up with oxygen or air, which is then delivered to the patient through the mask or tubing.</p><p>The Self Inflating Bag Market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing prevalence of respiratory diseases, trauma cases, and cardiac arrests.

<p><p>Voltage Variable Attenuators are electronic devices used to adjust the intensity of an electrical signal while maintaining the signal integrity. There are two main types of Voltage Variable Attenuators: Diode Based Attenuators and MMIC Based Attenuators.</p><p>Diode Based Attenuators use semiconductor diodes to control signal attenuation, providing high linearity and low insertion loss. On the other hand, MMIC Based Attenuators utilize Monolithic Microwave Integrated Circuits to offer precise and stable signal attenuation across a wide frequency range, making them ideal for high-performance applications.</p></p>

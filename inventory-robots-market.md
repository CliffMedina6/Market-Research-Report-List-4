<p><p>Inventory robots are classified into two main market types: mobile robotics and fixed robotics. Mobile robotics refer to robots that are able to move autonomously within a space, such as warehouses or retail stores, to perform inventory tasks. Fixed robotics, on the other hand, are stationary robots that are mounted in a specific location and perform inventory tasks within that confined area. Both types of robots are used to streamline and optimize inventory management processes in various industries.</p></p>

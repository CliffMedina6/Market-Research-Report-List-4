<p><p>Prokaryotic expression systems involve using bacteria to produce proteins, commonly used for fast and cost-effective protein production. Mammalian cell expression systems are ideal for producing complex proteins that require post-translational modifications. Yeast cell expression systems offer high protein yields and proper folding of proteins. Other protein expression systems include insect cell and plant cell expression systems, each suitable for specific protein production needs. These various types cater to different requirements in the protein expression service market, providing diverse options for researchers and industries.</p></p>

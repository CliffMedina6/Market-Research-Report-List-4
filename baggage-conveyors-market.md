<p><p>Baggage conveyors can be classified into three main types: belt conveyors, roller conveyors, and destination-coded vehicle (DCV) systems. </p><p>Belt conveyors use a continuous loop of material to move baggage, while roller conveyors use rollers to transport items. DCVs are autonomous vehicles that can transport baggage to designated locations based on coded information.</p><p>Each type of conveyor system has its own advantages and applications, catering to different needs and requirements in the baggage handling industry.</p></p>

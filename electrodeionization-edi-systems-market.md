<p><strong>The Electrodeionization (EDI) Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Homogeneous Membrane</li><li>Heterogeneous Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrodeionization (EDI) systems use both homogeneous and heterogeneous membranes to remove ions from water through an electrical current. In homogeneous membrane systems, the membrane material is consistent throughout, providing uniform ion removal. Heterogeneous membrane systems, on the other hand, utilize different materials in layers to target specific ions for removal. Both types of membranes play a crucial role in the purification process of water by efficiently removing ions and producing high-quality, pure water for various industrial applications.</p></p>

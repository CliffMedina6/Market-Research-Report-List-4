<p><p>Safety shut-off valves for gas meters are essential components in ensuring the safe operation of gas systems. Two common types are motorised valves and solenoid valves. Motorised valves are controlled by an electric motor, allowing for remote operation and precise control. Solenoid valves use an electromagnetic coil to open and close the valve, providing quick response times. Both types are commonly used in gas meter applications to prevent gas leaks and protect against potential hazards.</p></p>

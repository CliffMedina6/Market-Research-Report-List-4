<p><p>Applique scaling machines come in two main types: semi-automatic and CNC. </p><p>Semi-automatic machines require some manual input from operators, such as positioning the fabric and starting the machine. These machines are good for smaller scale operations or for those who may not have the budget for a fully automated system. </p><p>CNC machines are fully automated and use computer numerical control to precisely cut and scale appliques. These machines are ideal for high-volume production and offer greater accuracy and efficiency compared to semi-automatic machines.</p></p>

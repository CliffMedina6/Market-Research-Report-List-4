<p><p>Exterior Wall Systems include various market types such as Ventilated Façade, Curtain Walls, and Non-Ventilated Façade. Ventilated Façade systems consist of an outer layer that allows air to flow behind it, providing insulation and preventing moisture buildup. Curtain Walls are non-load-bearing walls that provide a building's exterior envelope, often made of glass or metal panels. Non-Ventilated Façade systems are traditional wall constructions without a cavity for air flow, typically consisting of materials like brick, stone, or stucco. Each system offers unique benefits and design possibilities for different building projects.</p></p>

Ventricular Assist Devices are implanted to help the heart pump blood to the rest of the body. Full Artificial Hearts are used to completely replace a failing heart. Intra-aortic Balloon Pumps are inserted into the aorta to help improve blood flow and oxygen delivery to the heart. Other devices in the market may include implantable pacemakers, defibrillators, and external heart pumps.</p></p>

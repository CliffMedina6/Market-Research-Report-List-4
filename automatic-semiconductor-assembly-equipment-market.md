<p><p>Fully automatic semiconductor assembly equipment refers to machines that can perform all tasks without human intervention, including loading and unloading of components, assembly, testing, and packaging. These systems are highly efficient and can significantly increase productivity in semiconductor manufacturing.</p><p>Semi-automatic semiconductor assembly equipment, on the other hand, require some level of human intervention to operate, such as feeding components into the machine or monitoring the assembly process. While not as fully automated as their counterparts, semi-automatic systems still offer increased efficiency and precision compared to manual assembly methods.</p></p>

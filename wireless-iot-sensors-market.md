<p><p>Traditional Wireless Technology in the IoT sensors market includes technologies like Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and Zigbee. These technologies provide high data transfer rates but are limited by range and battery life. LPWANs (Low-Power Wide Area Networks) technology, on the other hand, are designed specifically for IoT applications, offering long-range communication with low power consumption. LPWANs include technologies like LoRa and Sigfox, which are ideal for applications that require low data rates but extended coverage, such as smart city and agriculture applications.</p></p>

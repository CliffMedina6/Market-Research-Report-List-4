<p><p>Ported Short Peripheral Intravenous Catheters (PIVC) are catheters that have a port at the hub, allowing for the administration of multiple medications or fluids simultaneously. This type of catheter is commonly used in patients requiring frequent medication changes or those with limited peripheral access. Non-Ported Short PIVCs, on the other hand, do not have a port and are typically used for shorter durations or for single medication administration. Both types serve the purpose of facilitating venous access for medication administration or fluid delivery in a variety of healthcare settings.</p></p>

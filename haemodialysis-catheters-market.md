<p><ul><li>Cuffed Tunneled</li><li>Non-cuffed Tunneled</li><li>Non-tunneled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Haemodialysis catheters are used for temporary vascular access in patients undergoing dialysis. The market types include cuffed tunneled, non-cuffed tunneled, and non-tunneled catheters. Cuffed tunneled catheters have a cuff that helps secure the catheter in place for long-term use. Non-cuffed tunneled catheters are similar but do not have a cuff. Non-tunneled catheters are inserted directly into the vein without tunneling under the skin. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on the patient's specific needs and the duration of dialysis treatment.</p></p>

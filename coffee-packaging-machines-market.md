<p><p>Coffee packaging machines can be categorized into two main types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic machines are designed to perform all packaging processes without manual intervention, from measuring and filling to sealing and labeling. They are suitable for high-volume production and offer increased efficiency and consistency. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some manual operation, such as placing bags or containers and activating the machine. They are ideal for small to medium scale production and offer more flexibility and control over the packaging process.</p></p>

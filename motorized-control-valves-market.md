<p><p>Motorized control valves are available in various types including butterfly valves, ball valves, globe valves, and others. Butterfly valves are often used for large flow applications due to their quick operation and low pressure drop. Ball valves offer tight shut-off and precise control, making them suitable for a wide range of applications. Globe valves provide excellent throttling capabilities and are commonly used in HVAC systems. Other types of motorized control valves include diaphragm valves, pinch valves, and gate valves, each with their own unique advantages and applications.</p></p>

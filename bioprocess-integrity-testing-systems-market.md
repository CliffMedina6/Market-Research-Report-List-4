<p><p>Bioprocess Integrity Testing Systems market includes two main types - Destructive Testing and Non-Destructive Testing. Destructive Testing involves physically altering the sample to assess its integrity, often resulting in the destruction of the product. Non-Destructive Testing, on the other hand, allows for the assessment of integrity without altering or destroying the sample. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and reliability of bioprocess systems by detecting any potential defects or flaws.</p></p>

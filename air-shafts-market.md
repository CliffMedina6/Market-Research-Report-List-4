Leaf Shafts utilize a series of expanding leaves to hold the core securely. Strip Shafts clamp the core with strips that run along the length of the shaft. The "Others" category includes various special designs that cater to specific needs and applications. Each type offers unique features and benefits to accommodate different types of materials and requirements.</p></p>

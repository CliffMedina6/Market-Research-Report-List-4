<p><strong>The Mode Locker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Type</li><li>Passive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mode locker market includes two main types: active type and passive type. Active type mode lockers use an external power source to actively control the modulation of the laser pulses, offering precise control and fast response times. On the other hand, passive type mode lockers rely on materials with nonlinear optical properties to modulate the laser pulses without the need for an external power source. Passive mode lockers are known for their simplicity and reliability, making them suitable for various applications in the laser industry.</p></p>

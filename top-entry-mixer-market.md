Paddle mixers utilize rotating paddles to mix substances, while turbine mixers use blades or impellers for agitation. Magnetic mixers rely on magnetic coupling to drive the mixing process without any direct contact between the motor and the mixing vessel. Other markets refer to various types of top-entry mixers that do not fall under the three aforementioned categories, offering unique solutions for specific mixing requirements.</p></p>

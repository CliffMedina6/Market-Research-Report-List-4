<p><p>Hepatitis C testing market includes three main types: virus testing, which detects the presence of the virus in the blood; antibody testing, which looks for the body's immune response to the virus; and antigen testing, which identifies specific proteins on the surface of the virus. Each type of testing serves a different purpose in diagnosing and monitoring Hepatitis C infection. Virus testing is used to confirm active infection, antibody testing shows past or present exposure, and antigen testing helps in determining viral load.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>TEM Mode</li><li>TE Mode</li><li>TM Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dielectric resonators are used in various applications such as filters, oscillators, and antennas. They can be classified into three main types based on the modes of electromagnetic wave propagation within them: TEM mode (Transverse ElectroMagnetic mode), TE mode (Transverse Electric mode), and TM mode (Transverse Magnetic mode). Each mode has its own unique characteristics and applications. TEM mode resonators have a uniform electric and magnetic field distribution, while TE and TM mode resonators have either purely electric or magnetic field distribution, respectively.</p></p>

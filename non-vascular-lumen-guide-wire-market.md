<p><p>The non-vascular lumen guide wire market consists of two main types: straight guidewires and elbow guidewires. Straight guidewires are used for straightforward insertion through narrow, tortuous pathways, while elbow guidewires have a pre-formed curve at the distal end to navigate sharp bends and twists in the anatomy. Each type of guidewire offers specific advantages depending on the complexity of the procedure and anatomy being navigated, providing physicians with versatile tools for successful catheter placement and treatment.</p></p>

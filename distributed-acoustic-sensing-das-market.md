<p><p>Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) is a technology that converts fiber optic cables into a network of acoustic sensors for monitoring and detecting sound waves. The DAS market includes two main types: Distributed Acoustic Sensing Interrogator (DASI) and Distributed Acoustic Sensing Processor (DASP). DASI products focus on providing advanced sensing capabilities for a wide range of applications, while DASP products are designed to process and analyze the data collected by DASI systems, enabling real-time monitoring and detection of acoustic events.</p></p>

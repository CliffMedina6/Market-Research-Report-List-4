<p><p>Cast Acrylic Sheets are available in two main types: Cell Cast Acrylic Sheet and Continuous Cast Acrylic Sheet. Cell Cast Acrylic Sheets are produced by pouring liquid acrylic into molds, allowing it to harden, and then polishing the surface for clarity. Continuous Cast Acrylic Sheets are made by continuously pouring liquid acrylic between two sheets of glass, creating a smooth and uniform surface. Both types of cast acrylic sheets offer excellent clarity, durability, and a wide range of applications in various industries.</p></p>

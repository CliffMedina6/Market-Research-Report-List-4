<p><p>Medical cleaners are categorized into four main types based on their chemical composition and cleaning properties. Alkaline cleaners are effective at removing organic materials and fats. Neutral cleaners are gentle and non-corrosive, suitable for everyday cleaning tasks. Acidic cleaners are useful for removing mineral deposits and disinfecting surfaces. Enzyme cleaners contain enzymes that break down organic matter, making them ideal for cleaning biological stains. Each type of cleaner serves a specific cleaning purpose in medical facilities to ensure a clean and hygienic environment.</p></p>

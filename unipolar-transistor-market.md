<p><p>Unipolar transistors are classified into two main types: Insulated Gate Field Effect Transistor (IGFET) and Junction Field Effect Transistor (JFET). IGFETs are known for their high input impedance and low power consumption, making them ideal for digital applications. On the other hand, JFETs have a simpler structure and are better suited for analog switching applications. Both types of transistors play a vital role in the semiconductor industry, catering to the diverse needs of electronic devices.</p></p>

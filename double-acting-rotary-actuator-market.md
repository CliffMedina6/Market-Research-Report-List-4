<p><p>Double-acting Rotary Actuators are commonly available in two main types: Balanced Rotary Actuators and Rack-and-pinion Pneumatic Rotary Actuators. Balanced Rotary Actuators use pressurized air to create a balanced torque output, allowing for precise control and smooth operation. On the other hand, Rack-and-pinion Pneumatic Rotary Actuators use a rack and pinion mechanism to convert linear motion into rotary motion, providing a cost-effective and reliable solution for various industrial applications. Each type offers unique advantages and is used in different scenarios based on specific requirements.</p></p>

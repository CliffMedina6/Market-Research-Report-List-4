<p><p>Dental wax market encompasses various types of wax used in dentistry. Pattern wax is used by dentists to create accurate models of teeth for crowns and bridges. Processing wax assists in the fabrication of dental restorations. Impression wax helps make detailed molds of a patient's teeth for accurate fittings. Healing wax is used post-surgery to protect wounds and aid in healing. Each type of wax serves a specific purpose in the dental field, catering to different aspects of dental care.</p></p>

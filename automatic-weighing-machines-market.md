<p><p>Automatic weighing machines are used in various industries to accurately measure the weight of products. In-Motion Checkweighers are designed to continuously weigh products as they move along a conveyor belt, ensuring efficient and real-time monitoring of production. On the other hand, Intermittent Checkweighers are used to weigh products in batches, pausing between each measurement. Both types of checkweighers play a crucial role in quality control and ensuring compliance with regulations in industries such as food and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>

Single Suction pumps have one inlet for water intake, while Double Suction pumps have two inlets for balanced water pressure. Single Suction pumps are commonly used in smaller industrial boilers, while Double Suction pumps are preferred for larger boilers that require higher water flow rates. Both types are crucial for maintaining efficient boiler operations in industrial settings.</p></p>

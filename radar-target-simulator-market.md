<p><p>Radar Target Simulator Market Types can be classified into two main categories - Benchtop RTES and Portable RTES. Benchtop RTES are stationary systems typically used in laboratory settings for testing and calibration of radar systems. They offer higher processing power and connectivity options. On the other hand, Portable RTES are smaller, more compact systems that are suitable for field testing and on-site troubleshooting. They are easy to transport and set up, making them ideal for military, defense, and security applications.</p></p>

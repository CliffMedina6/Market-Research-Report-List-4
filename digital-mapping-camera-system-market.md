<p><strong>The Digital Mapping Camera System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Array Scanners (Pushbroom)</li><li>Systems based on Area Arrays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Linear array scanners, also known as pushbroom scanners, use a single line of sensors to capture images as the camera moves along a path. These systems are ideal for applications such as satellite imaging and aerial photography. On the other hand, systems based on area arrays utilize multiple sensors arranged in a grid pattern to capture images simultaneously. This type of technology is commonly used in digital cameras and smartphones to capture high-quality images quickly and efficiently. Both types of systems play a crucial role in the digital mapping camera market for various applications.</p></p>

<p><p>Stepped thermowells are available in various types to suit different applications. The market includes flanged tapered thermowells, threaded tapered thermowells, socket weld tapered thermowells, vanstone tapered thermowells, and weld-in socketed type tapered thermowells. Flanged tapered thermowells are secured to a flange, threaded ones are screwed into a fitting, socket weld thermowells are welded to a pipe, vanstone thermowells are clamped onto a pipe with a van stone flange, and weld-in socketed thermowells are welded into a socket. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the specific requirements of the installation.</p></p>

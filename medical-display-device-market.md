<p><p>The medical display device market consists of two main types: LED and OLED displays. LED displays use light-emitting diodes to produce images, providing high brightness and energy efficiency. OLED displays, on the other hand, use organic light-emitting diodes to create images with better color accuracy and contrast ratio compared to LED displays. Both types are widely used in medical imaging applications such as X-rays, MRIs, and ultrasounds to provide accurate and detailed visual information to healthcare professionals.</p></p>

<p><p>Civil Aircraft Weighing Equipment market types include Platform System and Jack Weigh System. Platform System involves placing the aircraft on weighing platforms to determine its weight distribution accurately. On the other hand, Jack Weigh System uses jacks to lift different parts of the aircraft individually for weight measurement. Both systems are crucial for ensuring the safety and efficiency of aircraft operations by providing accurate weight information for maintenance, loading, and performance calculations.</p></p>

<p><p>Wellhead valves are essential components in oil and gas production operations, and they are available in two main types: manual and automatic. Manual wellhead valves require human intervention for operation, allowing for hands-on control of the flow of fluids. On the other hand, automatic wellhead valves are operated using sensors and control systems, enabling remote control and monitoring of the flow. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and efficiency of wellhead operations.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Hammer Mills</li><li>Roller Mills</li><li>Pellet Mills</li><li>Flaking Mills</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Feed mill market consists of various types of milling equipment used for processing feed ingredients. Hammer mills are used for grinding grains and creating fine particle sizes. Roller mills crush grains into smaller particles for better digestibility. Pellet mills compress ingredients into pellets for easier handling and storage. Flaking mills are used for flaking grains to increase surface area and improve digestibility. Each type of mill caters to different processing needs in the feed industry, offering unique advantages and applications.</p></p>

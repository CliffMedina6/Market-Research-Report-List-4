<p><ul><li>Master Alarm</li><li>Area Alarm</li><li>Local Alarm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Master Alarm systems are used to monitor the overall status of the medical gas system and provide a centralized alert for any issues detected. Area Alarm systems are installed in specific areas of a healthcare facility to monitor gas levels and ensure safety within that designated space. Local Alarm systems are typically connected to individual gas outlets or valves to provide immediate alerts in case of any abnormalities. Each type of alarm system plays a crucial role in maintaining the safety and functionality of medical gas systems in healthcare settings.</p></p>

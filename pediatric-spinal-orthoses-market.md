<p><p>Pediatric spinal orthoses market includes two main types: dynamic orthotics and static orthotics. Dynamic orthotics are designed to provide adjustable support and realignment for growing spines, using moving parts to facilitate movement while still offering stabilization. Static orthotics, on the other hand, provide rigid support and immobilization for specific conditions or injuries. Both types play a crucial role in the treatment and management of spinal issues in children, offering different levels of support and functionality based on individual needs.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Head-Up Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Windshield Projected HUD</li><li>Combiner Projected HUD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Head-Up Display (HUD) market includes two main types: Windshield Projected HUD and Combiner Projected HUD. In Windshield Projected HUD, information such as speed, navigation directions, and other relevant data are projected directly onto the windshield, allowing the driver to view it without taking their eyes off the road. Combiner Projected HUD, on the other hand, projects the information onto a transparent display located in front of the driver. Both types of HUDs aim to enhance driving safety and convenience by providing vital information in the driver's line of sight.</p></p>

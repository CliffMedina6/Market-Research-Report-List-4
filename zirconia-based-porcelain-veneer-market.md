<p><p>Zirconia-based porcelain veneers can be categorized into two main types based on their composition and construction: No Layering Porcelain and Layering with Porcelain. No Layering Porcelain veneers are made from a single solid layer of zirconia-based porcelain, providing a natural and translucent appearance. On the other hand, Layering with Porcelain veneers consist of multiple layers of porcelain that are built up to mimic the natural translucency and color variations of real teeth. Both types of veneers offer excellent esthetic results and durability.</p></p>

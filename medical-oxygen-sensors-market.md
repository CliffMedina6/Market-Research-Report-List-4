Zirconia oxygen sensors utilize a ceramic material to measure the oxygen concentration in a gas, while optical oxygen sensors use light to determine oxygen levels. Both types are essential in medical settings to ensure accurate and efficient oxygen therapy for patients. They play a significant role in maintaining the health and well-being of individuals relying on supplementary oxygen.</p></p>

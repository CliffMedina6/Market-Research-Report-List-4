<p><p>Fire dampers are devices used in HVAC systems to prevent the spread of fire and smoke through ductwork in a building. There are two main types of fire dampers in the market: manual fire dampers and motorized fire dampers. Manual fire dampers are operated by hand and are typically opened or closed during maintenance inspections. Motorized fire dampers are equipped with automatic actuators that close the damper in response to a fire alarm signal. Both types play a crucial role in fire safety and building protection.</p></p>

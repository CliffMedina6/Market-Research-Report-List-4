<p><p>Airport tugs are used to tow aircrafts and can be classified into two main types: Conventional towbars and Towbarless tugs. </p><p>Conventional towbars require a towbar to be attached to the nose landing gear of the aircraft for towing, whereas towbarless tugs are designed to lift and tow aircraft without the need for a towbar. </p><p>Both types of tugs have their own advantages and are chosen based on factors such as aircraft size, weight, and the specific requirements of the airport.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Multipass Gas Cell Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pfund Cell</li><li>White Cell</li><li>Herriott Cell</li><li>Circular Multipass Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multipass gas cells are commonly used in spectroscopic analysis to increase the path length of light through a sample. There are four main types of multipass gas cells on the market: Pfund cell, White cell, Herriott cell, and Circular multipass cell. Pfund cell utilizes a fixed mirror arrangement, White cell has adjustable mirrors for path optimization, Herriott cell uses two concave mirrors for multiple reflections, and Circular multipass cell has a circular geometry for efficient light trapping. Each type offers unique benefits and can be tailored to specific experimental needs.</p></p>

<p><p>Dental bone grafts and substitutes market primarily consists of demineralized bone matrix, xenograft, and synthetic products. Demineralized bone matrix is derived from human cadavers and contains growth factors to promote bone regeneration. Xenografts are derived from animal sources and are processed to remove immunogenic components. Synthetic bone graft substitutes are made from materials like calcium phosphate and promote new bone growth. These products are used in dental procedures like socket preservation and ridge augmentation to enhance bone regeneration and support dental implants.</p></p>

<p><p>Industrial Grade Optical Network Units (ONU) are essential components in optical network infrastructure. There are two main types of ONU in the market: Active Optical Network Units and Passive Optical Network Units. </p><p>Active ONUs are equipped with electronic components that enable signal processing and data transmission, making them suitable for high-speed networks and long-distance communication. </p><p>Passive ONUs, on the other hand, do not have active components and rely on splitting and distributing optical signals without any signal regeneration, making them more cost-effective and energy-efficient for shorter distances and lower bandwidth applications.</p></p>

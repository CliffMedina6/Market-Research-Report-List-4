<p><p>Resilient metal seals are used in various industries for their ability to withstand high temperatures, pressure, and corrosive environments. Two common types of resilient metal seals are butadiene rubber seals and fluoroelastomer seals. Butadiene rubber seals are known for their flexibility and resistance to abrasion, making them ideal for applications requiring a tight seal. On the other hand, fluoroelastomer seals are highly resistant to chemicals and extreme temperatures, making them suitable for demanding industrial environments. Both types of seals play a crucial role in ensuring leak-free connections and preventing contamination.</p></p>

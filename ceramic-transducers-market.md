<p><p>Ceramic transducers are commonly classified into two main types: piezoelectric and others. Piezoelectric transducers utilize the piezoelectric effect to convert electrical energy into mechanical vibration or vice versa. These transducers are widely used in various applications such as sensors, actuators, and medical devices. On the other hand, the "other" market for ceramic transducers includes various types such as capacitive, resistive, and magnetostrictive transducers, each serving specific needs in industries like automotive, aerospace, and consumer electronics.</p></p>

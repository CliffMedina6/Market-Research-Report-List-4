<p><p>Radiation measuring instruments can be categorized into different types based on their technology and purpose. Scintillation detectors are used to detect and measure radiation by converting the energy of radiation into light pulses. Neutron detectors are specifically designed to measure neutron radiation. Geiger counters are widely used for detecting and measuring ionizing radiation. Other types of radiation measuring instruments may include proportional counters, ionization chambers, and semiconductor detectors. Each type of instrument serves a specific purpose in the monitoring and detection of radiation levels.</p></p>

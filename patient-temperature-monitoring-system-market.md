<p><p>Patient temperature monitoring system market is divided into two main types: non-invasive and invasive temperature monitoring. </p><p>Non-invasive temperature monitoring involves using external sensors to measure the patient's temperature without penetrating the skin. This method is commonly used for quick and easy temperature monitoring in clinical settings.</p><p>Invasive temperature monitoring, on the other hand, involves inserting a probe directly into the patient's body to obtain accurate temperature readings. This method is typically used in critical care situations where precise temperature monitoring is necessary.</p></p>

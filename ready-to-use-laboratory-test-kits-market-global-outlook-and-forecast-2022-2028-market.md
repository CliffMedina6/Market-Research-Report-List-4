<p><p>The ready-to-use laboratory test kits market includes various types such as agglutination assay, immune-chromatographic techniques, immune-dot techniques, immune-filtration techniques, flow-through, lateral flow, and solid phase. Agglutination assay involves the clumping of particles, while immune-chromatographic techniques utilize antibodies for detection. Immune-dot techniques use a dot matrix format for testing, and immune-filtration techniques involve antibody capture on a porous membrane. Flow-through tests involve liquid flow for detection, while lateral flow tests provide results through capillary action. Solid phase tests utilize a solid support for antibody binding.</p></p>

<p><p>CNC lathes can be classified into two main types based on their orientation: vertical and horizontal. Vertical CNC lathes have the spindle in a vertical position, allowing for easy loading and unloading of workpieces. These machines are commonly used for intricate and complex workpieces. On the other hand, horizontal CNC lathes have the spindle in a horizontal position, making them suitable for heavy-duty cutting operations. They are often used in industries like automotive and aerospace for mass production of components.</p></p>

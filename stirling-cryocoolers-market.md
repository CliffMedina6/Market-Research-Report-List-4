<p><strong>The Stirling Cryocoolers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Cryocoolers</li><li>Rotary Cryocoolers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stirling Cryocoolers are categorized into two main types based on their operation: Linear Cryocoolers and Rotary Cryocoolers. Linear Cryocoolers work by cycling a working gas back and forth in a linear motion, while Rotary Cryocoolers operate by rotating a displacer or regenerator in a circular motion. Both types of cryocoolers are used for cooling applications in scientific research, medical imaging, electronics, and aerospace industries. Linear Cryocoolers are known for their simplicity and reliability, whereas Rotary Cryocoolers are preferred for their higher efficiency and compact size.</p></p>

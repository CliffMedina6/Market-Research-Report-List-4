<p><p>Rim boards are essential components of the construction industry used in framing and supporting floor joists. There are various market types for rim boards, including plywood, oriented strand board (OSB), glued laminated timber (glulam), and laminated veneer lumber (LVL). Plywood rim boards are made from thin sheets of wood veneer, OSB rim boards are composed of compressed wood strands, glulam rim boards are crafted from layers of solid timber glued together, and LVL rim boards are constructed from multiple layers of veneer bonded with adhesives.</p></p>

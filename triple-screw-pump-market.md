<p><p>Triple screw pumps are available in three main market types based on their mounting orientation: horizontal, flange, and vertical. Horizontal mounting is widely used in industrial applications where space is limited and a compact system is required. Flange mounting is commonly used in applications where the pump needs to connect directly to a pipeline. Vertical mounting is ideal for applications where there is a need to pump liquids from a lower level to a higher level, such as in oil refineries or chemical plants.</p></p>

<p><p>Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) Equipment Market includes various types such as Cathodic Arc Vapor Deposition, Electron Beam Vapor Deposition, Sputter Deposition, and others. Cathodic Arc Vapor Deposition involves the use of an electric arc to vaporize the cathode material. Electron Beam Vapor Deposition utilizes an electron beam to evaporate materials for coating. Sputter Deposition involves bombarding the target material with high-energy ions to create a thin film. Other types include thermal evaporation and ion plating, each with unique characteristics and applications in the PVD equipment market.</p></p>

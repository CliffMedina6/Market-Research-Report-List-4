<p><p>Auto Darkening (ADF) Welding Helmets come in two main types: Fixed Shade and Variable Shade. Fixed Shade helmets have a set darkening level that does not change, while Variable Shade helmets allow the user to adjust the shade level depending on the welding process and conditions. Fixed Shade helmets are more affordable and easier to use for beginners, while Variable Shade helmets offer more flexibility and customization for experienced welders. Both types provide protection from harmful UV and IR radiation during welding.</p></p>

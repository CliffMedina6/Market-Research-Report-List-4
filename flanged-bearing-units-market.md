<p><p>Flanged bearing units are available in various types such as ball bearing units, plain sleeve bearing units, and roller bearing units. Ball bearing units consist of a ball bearing housed in a flanged housing, providing high precision and load carrying capacity. Plain sleeve bearing units use a cylindrical sleeve instead of balls for smooth movement and reduced friction. Roller bearing units incorporate cylindrical or tapered rollers for enhanced load carrying capacity and durability. Each type offers unique advantages for different application requirements in various industries.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Wet Plaster Trimmer</li><li>Dry Plaster Trimmer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plaster trimmer market can be divided into two types: wet plaster trimmers and dry plaster trimmers. Wet plaster trimmers use water to help control dust and prevent overheating during the trimming process. They are typically used for softer materials like gypsum. Dry plaster trimmers do not use water and are better suited for harder materials like cement. Both types of trimmers offer different benefits depending on the material being worked with, making them suitable for a variety of applications in the construction and medical industries.</p></p>

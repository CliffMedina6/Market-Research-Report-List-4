<p><p>Mosquito killing lamps come in various types to cater to different preferences. Electric shock type lamps attract mosquitos with UV light and kill them with an electric shock. Sticky trap type lamps use adhesive surfaces to trap mosquitos. Airflow type lamps create strong air currents to suck in mosquitos. Photocatalysis type lamps use a reaction between UV light and a titanium dioxide coating to trap and kill mosquitos. Each type offers a unique approach to effectively rid areas of pesky mosquitos.</p></p>

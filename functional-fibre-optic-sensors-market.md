<p><strong>The Functional Fibre Optic Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intrinsic Sensors</li><li>Extrinsic Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Functional fiber optic sensors are available in two main types - intrinsic sensors and extrinsic sensors. </p><p>Intrinsic sensors are integrated directly into the fiber optic cable itself, allowing for the accurate measurement of parameters such as temperature, pressure, or strain along the entire length of the cable. </p><p>Extrinsic sensors, on the other hand, are separate from the fiber optic cable and are used to measure external variables by transmitting and receiving signals through the fiber optic system. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in various industries such as aerospace, oil and gas, and biomedical applications.</p></p>

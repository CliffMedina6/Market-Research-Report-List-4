<p><p>Translucent glass is a type of glass that allows light to pass through while diffusing it, creating a soft, diffused glow rather than clear visibility. This technology offers enhanced privacy and control over light and heat transmission, making it an attractive option for modern architectural designs. Another trend is the use of patterned or textured translucent glass, which adds a decorative element to the glass while still allowing light to pass through.</p></p>

<p><p>Translucent glass market types include float glass and rolled glass. Float glass is made by floating molten glass on a bed of molten tin, resulting in a smooth and uniform surface. Rolled glass is manufactured by passing glass between rollers to create different textures and patterns. Both types of glass are popular for their ability to allow light to pass through while still providing privacy. They are commonly used in building and interior design applications.</p></p>

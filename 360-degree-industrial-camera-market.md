<p><p>In the 360-degree industrial camera market, there are two main types: spherical 360 cameras and panoramic 360 cameras. Spherical 360 cameras capture a full 360-degree view in all directions, providing a truly immersive experience for the viewer. On the other hand, panoramic 360 cameras capture a wide horizontal view of up to 360 degrees, but may not provide a full spherical view. Both types of cameras are used in various industrial applications such as surveillance, monitoring, and virtual tours.</p></p>
